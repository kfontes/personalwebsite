About

I grew up in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, and while I miss the beaches and amazing Lebanese food, I am happy to call the PNW my new home.

I am a software engineering Masters student at Seattle University, interested in building and applying technology to meaningful issues in the world.

The perfect day for me would would involve me getting my hands dirty with paint or pastels, going for a hike in the beautiful PNW, and eating a spicy black bean burger with sweet potato fries chased down by a cold local beer.
